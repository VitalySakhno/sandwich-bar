Our Story

Crafting the Perfect Sandwich Since 2024

Nestled in the heart of Shrewsbury, Coleham Deli & Sandwich Bar is a traditional British sandwich shop where passion meets quality. We believe that a great sandwich starts with exceptional ingredients and time-honored methods.

Every single morning, our kitchen comes alive with the aroma of freshly baked bread. From our signature crusty paninis to classic breakfast rolls, we proudly source 100% of our meats, cheeses, and vegetables from local Shropshire farmers and artisans.
